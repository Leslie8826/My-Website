Computer Science

During Summer 2019, I was a course assistant in CS in the GSTEM program at NYU Courant. Thanks to the program, high-schoolers complete internships in different labs in the NYC area.

My main duties consisted in giving students the required CS knowledge they needed during their internship (one on one tutoring to understand some CS concepts) and providing them guidance during their scientific journey.

The internships' subjects were very varied and could cover machine learning applied to quantum chemistry or aerospace engineering, data science algorithms in wireless networks, biomedical engineering, or cybersecurity.

Also, the GSTEM program includes weekly trips to tech giants, hedge funds and major labs where students can learn about current employees about their STEM experience.
