Anecdote on CrossFit

44 minutes and 56 seconds as I look at my watch with proudness after completing the MURPH for time.

As a reminder the MURPH in CrossFit is 1 mile run, 100 pull-ups, 200 push-ups, 300 squats, 1 mile run.

I had that big smile on my face because 4 months earlier, I tried the MURPH for the first time. It was my second CrossFit session and it was very hard. The cap time to finish was 60 minutes. By the sixtieth minute I was still in the middle of the push-ups, exhausted.

The day after this second CrossFit session, all of my muscles were so sore that I had a hard time doing the simplest moves. I recall thinking: "They are all crazy those CrossFitters!!!"

Then I quickly shifted my mind into how I could do better for my next MURPH. I acknowledged that I was not prepared at all for such a high intensity training and focused on working on my weaknesses.
