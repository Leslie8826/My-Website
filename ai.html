Artificial Intelligence

I had the chance to teach AI to a group of high-schoolers in summer 2018 at the Center For K12 STEM Education at NYU Tandon School of Engineering. The center offers different programs during the summer.

The experience was amazing and challenging. In a short amount of time, we had to put up a program with my PhD-student colleague based on his research. We spent a lot of hours choosing the required content for our chosen chapters on each topic, testing each in-class experiment, deciding what to add last-minute or remove and so on.

In the end, it was all worth it: everyone in the team (my colleague and the other instructors teaching other subjects) was very supportive. Not only I learnt a lot myself on AI but we could transfer our knowledge to our high-schoolers audience. The topics in our AI program was:

  • Electronics
  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Mathematics
  • Artificial Neural Networks
  • Convolution
  • Convolutional Neural Networks
  • Introduction to Python: see a part of the coding lessons here
