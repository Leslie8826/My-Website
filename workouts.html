My workouts

In general, I practise a lot of different sports and try to vary my workouts as often as possible depending on the target (steady weight, fat loss or muscle mass gain among others).

Below is a sample of my weekly workouts:

Saturday
  • 60 minutes of cardio (run or bike or mix of run/bike)
  • 5 x 20 reps of abs
  • 4 x 20 reps of side plank (2 sets of 20 reps for each side)
  • 2 x 20 seconds of forearm plank
  • 2 x 20 seconds of straight-arm plank

Sunday
  • 5 minutes warm-up
  • 3 x 10 reps of air squats
  • 3 x 10 reps of push-ups
  • 2 x 10 reps of lateral arm raises (5 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x Supersets of:
    • 1 x 10 of reps air squats
    • 1 x 10 of reps cross-body dumbbell hammer curl (15 pounds per dumbbell)
    • 1 x 10 of reps dumbbell curl (15 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x Supersets of:
    • 1 x 10 of reps air squats
    • 1 x 10 of reps cross-body dumbbell hammer curl (20 pounds per dumbbell)
    • 1 x 10 of reps dumbbell curl (20 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x Supersets of:
    • 1 x 10 of reps air squats
    • 1 x 10 of reps cross-body dumbbell hammer curl (25 pounds per dumbbell)
    • 1 x 10 of reps dumbbell curl (25 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x 10 reps of dumbbell airlift (25 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x 10 reps of lateral arm raises (7.5 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x 10 reps of dumbbell deadlift (30 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 1 x 10 reps of leg extension (50 pounds)
  • 1 x 10 reps of leg extension (60 pounds)
  • 1 x 10 reps of leg extension (70 pounds)
  • 1 x 10 reps of leg extension (80 pounds)
  • 1 x 10 reps of leg extension (90 pounds)
  • 2 x 10 reps of hamstring curl (35 pounds)
  • 2 x 10 reps of hamstring curl (40 pounds)
  • 1 x 10 reps of hamstring curl (45 pounds)

Rest day

  • 2 x Supersets of:

    • 20 seconds of triceps kick-back hold (20 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x 10 reps of lateral arm raises (7.5 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 2 x 10 reps of lateral arm raises (10 pounds per dumbbell)
  • 1 x 10 reps of two-arms triceps extension (20 pounds)
  • 1 x 10 reps of two-arms triceps extension (25 pounds)
