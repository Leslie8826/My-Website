Good Deeds

There are so many people in New York who cannot afford to eat.

I have always felt like I want to help in some way.

Whenever possible and with the permission of the party organizers, it happens that I take the food leftovers after a work party to distribute them to people in need.

This is a voluntary initiative from my part because I don't see why we would throw away food when there are so many individuals in the city who wonder what they will eat in the day.

In the future, I would like to do more than those occasional initiatives.
