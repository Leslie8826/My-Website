Scratch Programming

Scratch is a free programming language where anyone can create their own animations or games.

I started teaching Scratch in Fall 2019 to middle schoolers. Since my students are younger than the audience I used to working with, I tried to find a fun way to combine programming and creativity.

Also, Scratch is a new program I implemented at the schools I currently worked at.
